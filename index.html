About us ▼

This website is designed to enable the conversion of various file formats to MP4 and to upgrade videos to HD quality, ranging from 780p to 4K. It also provides the option to adjust, increase, decrease, or mute the audio volume of the selected video. One of the key focuses of this website is to repair videos that may have been corrupted. With the first ever website to detect multiple languages within a video, the AI will identify them and present a pop-up window offering you the following options:

Add To Audio Track: If the AI detects a specific language, you can choose to add it as an audio track and adjust the volume using a Corrupted Volume slider. This slider allows you to raise, lower, or completely mute the volume for the selected language track.

Remove Languages: If you wish to remove a particular language, you can select to delete it entirely from the video. Once this option is chosen, you will receive a new version of the video containing only the languages you wish to keep.

These features provide users with full control over the video and audio experience, ensuring that videos can be repaired and customized according to desired quality and language preferences.
